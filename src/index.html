Hello! I'm Thomas Kwashnak! I've created this website from the ground up to put on display my many achievements and work! I hope you stick around to learn more about me, what I like to do, and the things I've learned!

I currently study at the beautiful campus of Quinnipiac University as an Undergraduate Student in the Class of 2024. I came into Quinnipiac studying Computer Science, and quickly added an Economics Minor. Through the economics department, I was exposed to the opportunity to double major with Data Science, which I happily took. I've spent my years going above and beyond to try and teach myself as much as I can. Many of the projects listed below in my projects section are from courses I took at Quinnipiac!

Technology has been something I knew I loved since the day I first started using computers. I had the unique situation of growing up on Ubuntu Linux, which made me an avid user of Open Source and Linux. Currently, I run some form of linux (such as Pop!_OS) on my laptop, and Windows on my desktop (only for the games).

I love customizability, which is why my current editor of choice is Neovim! However, I won't try and sell it to you. Everyone has their own preferences, and there's no best editor. I think the most important factor is learning your editor. Think of it like driving your car; you may be able to drive most cars, but you become an expert in driving your own car well.

If you want to see how I've set up my neovim, you can find all my configurations on my dotfiles! For your own sanity, use this as a resource and don't just copy / paste willy nilly; I have some very specific tastes.
